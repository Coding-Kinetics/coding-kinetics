Amanda Hinchman-Dominguez, President

Welcome to Kotlin Kinetics, LLC! Kotlin Kinetics is a minority-owned, woman-owned business based in Chicago and globally online.

Kotlin Kinetics is run by Amanda Hinchman-Dominguez, a Kotlin GDE and co-author of the book Programming Android with Kotlin: Acheiving Structured Concurrency with Coroutines.

Amanda is active in the Kotlin and Android communities locally and globally. She is a regular speaker for KotlinConf, Droidcon, and other technical conferences since 2018, giving lectures across multiple cities including Amsterdam, Belarus, Budapest, Berlin, Copenhagen, and London, NY, San Francisco and Chicago.
